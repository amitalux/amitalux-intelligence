              Admin stale data alerts

              Amitalux Intelligence watches for customer records that no longer look trustworthy.

              The system can alert admins when customer records have not been verified, conflict with Square or CRM data, or contain unresolved notes that could lead to a poor customer experience.

              CRM painpoints covered

              Amitalux Intelligence is designed to avoid the problems that make teams hate traditional CRMs.

              The product should act like a system of intelligence, not a data-entry burden. The AI captures context, keeps records clean, connects tools, and helps the user in the moment.

              Manual data entry

              Covered. Amitalux Intelligence uses email lookup, conversation capture, AI summaries, handoff notes, and memory updates instead of asking reps to manually log everything.

              Overwhelming complexity

              Covered. The prototype is organized around the work: identify customer, detect emotion, show history, show options, and hand off clearly.

              Data rot

              Covered in the prototype with stale-record alerts. A production version should add duplicate detection, normalization, admin review queues, and merge tools.

              Integration silos

              Covered conceptually. Amitalux Intelligence can work standalone or connect with Square, Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Pipedrive, CSV import, OAuth, and API mapping.

              Passive reporting

              Covered. Amitalux Intelligence actively prepares replies, agent handoffs, customer memory, available resolutions, and business context.

              System of intelligence

              Covered as the core direction. Amitalux Intelligence senses emotion, remembers customer preferences, recommends next steps, and turns support into business action.

              Privacy and encryption

              Customer memory is powerful, so it has to be protected.

              Amitalux Intelligence models redaction, access controls, retention, audit logs, and end to end encryption boundaries.

              • AI receives redacted context unless a human approves more access.
              • Customer memory can be cleared or limited by consent.
              • Conversation payloads are modeled as encrypted before storage.
              • Every handoff, lookup, and AI action is audit logged.
              Connected context

              Amitalux Intelligence works standalone or with Square, Salesforce, and other CRMs.

              Integrations enrich the agent briefing with payment, booking, loyalty, case, account, and owner context.
