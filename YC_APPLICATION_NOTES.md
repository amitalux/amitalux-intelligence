# Amitalux Intelligence YC Application Notes

## Company

Amitalux Intelligence

## What Are You Building?

Amitalux Intelligence is an AI customer care platform for small service businesses. It helps businesses handle customer emails and chats with memory, emotional context, and follow-through, so customers feel remembered instead of treated like tickets.

## Problem

Small service businesses depend on repeat customers, but their support tools are either too simple or too heavy. They miss context, forget promises, respond inconsistently, and often make frustrated customers repeat themselves. Mature CRMs are powerful but too complex and expensive for many small teams.

## Product

Amitalux Intelligence has three connected surfaces:

- Customer Portal: customer-facing chat and email intake.
- Agent Portal: AI-assisted queue handling, customer context, reply drafting, approval, sending, and closing.
- Admin Portal: queue health, CSAT, case history, agent performance, stale data alerts, and audit trail.

## Why Now?

AI makes it possible for small businesses to deliver the kind of high-touch, remembered customer care that previously required a larger support team. Small businesses already have customer context in systems like Square, but they do not have a simple AI layer that turns that context into better service.

## Differentiation

Amitalux Intelligence is not another CRM. It is a care-first AI service agent focused on relationship repair.

Key differences:

- Relationship memory.
- Emotional context.
- Promise tracking.
- Human-sounding AI replies.
- Admin data health.
- Square-first small business context.
- Simple enough for small teams without consultants.

## Initial Customer

Small service businesses where repeat customer relationships matter:

- Salons and spas.
- Wellness studios.
- Boutique fitness.
- Med spas.
- Local service providers.
- Specialty retail with appointments or memberships.

## Business Model

SaaS subscription.

Possible early pricing:

- Starter: $49 to $99 per month.
- Growth: $149 to $299 per month.
- Premium: custom pricing for multi-location and advanced integrations.
