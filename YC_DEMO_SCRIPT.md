# Amitalux Intelligence YC Demo Script

Target length: 2 to 3 minutes.

## One-Sentence Opening

Amitalux Intelligence is an AI customer care platform for small service businesses that helps teams respond faster, sound more human, remember every customer, and repair service issues before they damage the relationship.

## Demo Flow

### 1. Customer Portal

Show the customer-facing side first.

Say:

"A customer can contact the business through chat or email. The message goes into the backend case queue, not just a fake front-end state."

Action:

- Open Customer Portal.
- Send the sample Maya billing message.

Point out:

- The message becomes a case.
- The customer-facing thread shows what the customer sent and what the business sends back.

### 2. Agent Portal

Say:

"The agent does not start from an empty ticket. Amitalux Intelligence brings in the customer profile, emotional context, history, and a response draft."

Action:

- Open Agent Portal.
- Select the new customer case.
- Click Run AI analysis.
- Review or edit the reply.
- Click Send reply.
- Click Close case.

Point out:

- The AI response avoids sounding robotic.
- The agent can edit before sending.
- The case timeline updates.
- The customer-facing reply appears back in the Customer Portal.

### 3. Admin Portal

Say:

"The admin sees the business operation, not just a pile of tickets."

Action:

- Open Admin Portal.

Point out:

- Queue health.
- CSAT.
- Handled volume.
- Agent performance.
- Stale customer data.
- Audit history.

## What To Emphasize

- Amitalux Intelligence is not trying to be Salesforce.
- It is care-first, not CRM-first.
- It is built for small service businesses that depend on repeat customers.
- The wedge is Square-first customer context.
- The longer-term moat is relationship memory, promise tracking, and repair intelligence.
