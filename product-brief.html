Product brief

Amitalux Intelligence

Amitalux Intelligence is an AI customer service agent and business assistant. When a customer gives their email address, Amitalux Intelligence pulls up the full customer record, detects emotional state through typing, and prepares the agent with the issue history, past resolutions, remaining options, and best response.

Open the prototype

What It Does

  • Identifies customers from an email address.
  • Includes customer profiles with consent, preferences, relationship value, memory, history, and available resolutions.
  • Includes agent profiles with workload, permissions, specialties, CSAT, response time, and AI coaching guidance.
  • Shows full customer profile, consent, channel preference, and relationship value.
  • Senses frustration or concern through customer typing.
  • Shows what resolutions were already offered.
  • Shows what solutions are still available.
  • Creates a chat or phone handoff note for a human agent.
  • Drafts a human-sounding response that does not feel automated.
  • Runs guided use cases that show the customer journey, queue impact, case progression, and business learning.
  • Shows reports for volume, queue health, CSAT, case progression, agent performance, and history.
  • Uses red, yellow, and green status indicators so admins can see what is healthy, at risk, or urgent.
  • Alerts admins when customer records look stale, conflicted, or unresolved.
  • Works standalone or with Square, Salesforce, and other CRM systems.

Functioning Browser Demo

The Live Workbench is interactive. A reviewer can select a customer case, run AI analysis, approve the reply, send the response, close the case, and add a new incoming case. The queue, history, CSAT, case progression, handled count, and reports update inside the browser as the work happens.

The current build now includes a Netlify backend. Customers, agents, cases, history, and live metrics are stored through serverless API routes instead of living only in the browser.

Product Portals

Amitalux Intelligence now has three product surfaces: a Customer Portal for customer-facing chat and email, an Agent Portal for handling those conversations, and an Admin Portal for queue health, reporting, agent performance, data health, and audit history.

A message submitted from the Customer Portal enters the backend queue. The Agent Portal can analyze, approve, send, and close it. The Admin Portal reflects the same work through live operational reporting.

Working Use Cases

The demo includes realistic flows for a frustrated billing customer, a missing gift note, and a stale CRM conflict. Each flow shows how Amitalux Intelligence connects customer recognition, sentiment, queue health, AI response, case progression, history, and business learning.

Reports And Operations

Amitalux Intelligence includes an operational dashboard for the business. Admins can see today’s customer care health, incoming volume, queue pressure, channel mix, agent performance, CSAT, and case progression. The goal is to show what changed, why it changed, and what needs attention next.

Red, yellow, and green indicators make the system easy to scan. Green means healthy. Yellow means the business should watch it. Red means the issue needs action before it damages the customer experience.

Admin Data Health

Amitalux Intelligence can monitor customer records for stale or conflicting information. For example, if Square shows a recent booking but Salesforce still marks the customer as a renewal candidate, Amitalux Intelligence can alert an admin before the business sends the wrong message.

This matters because clean records protect both sides. Customers do not have to correct the company, and the team does not make decisions from old or incomplete data.

Why It Is Different

Most customer service tools show tickets. Amitalux Intelligence shows the customer relationship. It helps the business avoid making the customer repeat the story, and it gives the agent the emotional context needed to respond well.

CRM Painpoints Covered

  • Manual data entry: Amitalux Intelligence reduces logging by using email lookup, conversation capture, AI summaries, handoff notes, and customer memory.
  • Overwhelming complexity: Amitalux Intelligence is organized around the support workflow rather than large admin-heavy CRM screens.
  • Data rot: Amitalux Intelligence centralizes customer identity, consent, memory, and history. The prototype now includes stale-record alerts. A production version should add duplicate detection, formatting normalization, admin review queues, and merge tools.
  • Integration silos: Amitalux Intelligence can work standalone or connect to Square, Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Pipedrive, CSV import, OAuth, and API mapping.
  • Passive reporting: Amitalux Intelligence actively prepares responses, agent handoffs, next best options, and business context instead of only reporting what happened.
  • System of intelligence: Amitalux Intelligence senses emotion, remembers customer preferences, recommends next steps, and turns support patterns into business action.
