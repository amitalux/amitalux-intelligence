# Amitalux Intelligence Product Architecture

Amitalux Intelligence for Customer Care is an AI-first Customer Operations Platform.

The CRM is the system of record.
Amitalux Intelligence is the operating layer.

## Core Areas

- Customer Intelligence
- Case Management
- AI Operations
- Human Operations
- Business Administration
- Reporting and Learning

## Core Objects

- Workspaces
- Customers
- Cases
- Conversations
- Messages
- AI Memories
- Knowledge Articles
- Tasks
- Automations
- Promises
- Orders
- Users
- Roles
- AI Decisions
- Audit Logs
- Integrations

## AI Agent Responsibilities

The AI Agent should:

- identify the customer
- retrieve customer memory
- retrieve case history
- detect intent
- detect emotion
- detect urgency
- detect risk
- search knowledge
- recommend next action
- draft human-sounding replies
- calculate confidence
- explain reasoning
- escalate when needed
- create tasks
- track promises
- update customer memory
- log decisions

## Product Structure

- Customer Intake
- Agent Workspace
- Supervisor Operations
- Admin Settings
- AI Center
- Reporting

## Product Principle

Every screen should answer:

1. What does the AI know?
2. Why does the AI recommend this?
3. What should the human do next?

Traditional CRMs track records.
Help desks track tickets.
Chatbots answer questions.

Amitalux Intelligence operates the customer care workflow.
